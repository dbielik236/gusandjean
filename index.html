Gus & Jean

Acoustic Duo

A dynamic husband-and-wife acoustic duo bringing fresh energy to classic hits and contemporary favorites. Based in Southern NH/ME, they've captivated audiences for years with their creative interpretations, seamless harmonies, and undeniable chemistry. From James Taylor to Ed Sheeran, their music resonates with listeners of all ages.
