Gus & Jean

Acoustic Duo

A dynamic acoustic duo breathing new life into classic hits across decades and genres, this husband and wife team has been performing together since their high school days and recently celebrated 18 years of marriage! Based in southern New Hampshire, they have earned a reputation as one the region's premier acoustic acts.

Known for their upbeat rhythms, seamless harmonies, and creative interpretations of beloved songs, Gus and Jean bring a fresh perspective to every performance. Their repertoire spans from the heartfelt melodies of James Taylor and the soulful ballads of Whitney Houston to contemporary hits by Ed Sheeran, Dua Lipa, and Harry Styles. Drawing inspiration from diverse influences from George Harrison to Adele, Gus and Jean's music resonates with audiences of all ages.

Whether serenading intimate gatherings or commanding larger stages, Gus and Jean captivate listeners with their infectious energy and undeniable chemistry. With each strum of the guitar and each note they harmonize, they invite you to experience the magic of timeless music through their unique lens.
